What is CineGraph?

CineGraph maps over a million films into one interactive similarity graph. Pick a film and see the movies most like it, scored by the people they share — director, writer, cinematographer, composer, editor, cast — plus genre, keyword and era overlap.

Import your Letterboxd ratings to get personal movie recommendations, or trace the shortest path of connections from one film to another.
